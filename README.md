# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis — PwC Data Analytics Simulation

> Completed as part of the **PwC Switzerland Data Analytics Virtual Job Simulation** on Forage.

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement

A telecom company was experiencing high customer churn but lacked clarity on **who was churning, why, and when**. The goal was to analyze customer data and surface actionable retention insights for the business team.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) | Data cleaning & EDA |
| SQL | Querying and filtering customer segments |
| Power BI | Interactive dashboard & visualization |

---

## 📁 Dataset

- **Source:** PwC / Forage simulation dataset
- **Size:** 7,043 telecom customer records
- **Features:** Contract type, tenure, payment method, monthly charges, internet service, churn label

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- 📌 **Month-to-month** contract customers churn at **3× the rate** of annual subscribers (55% of churners)
- 📌 Customers with **< 1 year tenure** account for **31%** of all churn
- 📌 **Electronic check** users have the highest churn rate at **33.58%**
- 📌 **Fiber optic** internet users churn more than DSL users
- 📌 Estimated **15–20% churn reduction** possible by targeting high-risk segments with retention offers

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Offer **annual contract discounts** to month-to-month customers in their first 3 months
2. Target **electronic check users** with auto-pay incentives
3. Prioritize **senior citizens** (1,142 affected) with dedicated retention plans
